FAQ´s

Which Credit Card´s do you support?

BudgetMate proudly supports a wide range of payment methods to ensure a seamless and secure transaction experience. We currently accept payments via Mastercard, Visa, Bancontact, and PayPal. This allows you to choose the option that best suits your needs while enjoying the convenience and flexibility of our platform.

Can I pay per year?

At the moment, BudgetMate offers payments on a monthly basis. However, we’re excited to announce that annual payment options will be available soon! This upcoming feature will provide even greater flexibility and value for our users. Stay tuned for updates!

Where I find my invoice?

Your invoices are automatically sent to the email address associated with your BudgetMate account after each payment. Please check your inbox (and spam folder, if necessary) for the email containing your invoice. If you need further assistance, feel free to contact our support team.

I can´t login. What now?

If you're unable to log in, we recommend first double-checking your login credentials, such as your email and password. Make sure there are no typos or issues with case sensitivity. If you're still having trouble, you can reset your password or contact our support team for further assistance. We're here to help you get back on track!

Can I get a refund?

For monthly payments, we unfortunately do not offer refunds. However, if you have any concerns or issues with your subscription, please feel free to contact our support team, and we’ll do our best to assist you.

We’re committed to providing you with the best experience. If you have any questions, feedback, or need help, feel free to contact us. We look forward to hearing from you!
